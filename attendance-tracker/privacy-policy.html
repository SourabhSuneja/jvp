Privacy Policy

JVP Attendance Tracker

Last updated: 15 January 2026

Overview

JVP Attendance Tracker is an internal school application used by authorized teachers to record and manage daily student attendance. We are committed to protecting user privacy.

Information We Collect

The app does not collect personal information.

  • Student name and roll number (school records)
  • Attendance status
  • Class name and date

Authentication

Login sessions are stored locally on the device only for convenience. Passwords are never stored.

Device Permissions

The app does not request access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, or files.

Data Usage

Data is used solely for attendance management and internal reporting. No tracking, advertising, or analytics are performed.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. Access is limited to authorized school staff.

Children’s Privacy

Students do not use the app directly. The app is intended only for school staff.

Contact

For questions regarding this policy, please contact the school administration.
