Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the JVP Teachers mobile application. This application is exclusively developed for the teaching staff of Jamna Vidyapeeth School, located at 4C, New Loha Mandi Road, Jaipur.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our teachers and the students whose data is managed through this application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information collected and used within the app.

2. Information Collection

The JVP Teachers app is a management tool designed for internal school administrative use. We do not collect personal data from the general public. Data processed by the app includes:

  • Teacher Login Credentials: Securely provided by the school administration for authentication purposes.
  • Student Academic Data: Marks, exam results, and academic performance records entered by teachers.
  • Attendance Records: Daily student attendance data.
  • Syllabus & Blueprints: Academic planning content.

Note: The app does not collect any data automatically (such as location or advertising IDs) beyond what is explicitly entered by the authorized teacher for record-keeping purposes.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for the operational requirements of Jamna Vidyapeeth School:

  • To authenticate authorized teaching staff.
  • To maintain digital records of student attendance and academic progress.
  • To facilitate the management of syllabus and examination blueprints.
  • To allow class administrators to manage student rosters (adding or modifying student details).

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of school data seriously. The app utilizes industry-standard security measures:

  • Secure Database: All data is stored on a secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase) with strict access controls.
  • Authentication: Access is restricted to authorized personnel via secure login credentials.
  • Encryption: Data transmission between the app and the server is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL protocols.

Only authorized teachers and administrators of Jamna Vidyapeeth have access to modify the data relevant to their specific duties.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent any personal identification information to third parties.

Data is strictly used for internal school management. However, we utilize third-party services to support the application's infrastructure:

  • Supabase: Used as the backend database and authentication provider. Please refer to Supabase's privacy policy for details on their data security practices.

6. Children's Privacy

While this application manages data related to students (children under 18), the application itself is not intended for use by children. The exclusive users of this application are adult teaching staff employed by Jamna Vidyapeeth.

All student data entered into the system is done so by authorized school personnel in compliance with school regulations and educational record-keeping standards.
